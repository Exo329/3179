Global warming and CO2 emissions

Global average surface temperature

Global temperatures have been rising sharply since 1979 when we compare them to the average since 1850. This is clear evidence of global warming.

Global CO2 emissions and temperature change of cities

It is widely accepted that CO2 contributes to long term man made global warming. Although temperatures have been rising throughout the world, some countries are responsible for much more CO2 emissions per capita.
Furthermore, different countries have been experiencing different levels of temperature change. This illustrates disparities between which countries produce the most CO2 and which cities have been impacted the hardest by temperature increases.
